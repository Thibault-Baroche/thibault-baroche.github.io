Dashboard for restaurateurs

Imagine that you are a restaurateur and you own four restaurants in Paris. Over the past four years, you have been using a cash register system provided by a company - let's call it Tracker-System. This system allows servers to take orders and generate the bills. It also enables you to handle customer payments. Today, you are commissioning the Tracker System company to provide you with a report based on this data.

The objective is to centralize all the data in one place, allowing you to address several issues:
- How to track the turnover of your various restaurants?
- How to monitor the profitability of your restaurants?
- What has been the impact of Covid and the various closures on your restaurants?
- What is the occupancy rate of your restaurants throughout the year? By day of the week? By service?
- What are the categories of products that perform best in each restaurant? What is their impact on profit margins?
